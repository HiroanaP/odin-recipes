Lasagnas

Description :

A classic & popular Italian dish.

One of the World most popular dish and my personal favorite one !

Ingredients :

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonnings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps :

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions./li>
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
